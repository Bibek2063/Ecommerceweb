Product Description

The Apple AirPods Pro (2nd generation) feature Active Noise Cancellation that reduces unwanted background noise, and Adaptive Transparency that lets outside sounds in while reducing loud environmental noise. Designed to create an optimized fit—and to stay comfortable—they come with four pairs of silicone tips (XS, S, M, L) to fit a wide range of ears.

The MagSafe Charging Case delivers more than 24 hours of battery life and includes a speaker for locating with Find My and a lanyard loop. And with one-touch setup, your AirPods Pro connect magically to your iPhone or Apple Watch.
